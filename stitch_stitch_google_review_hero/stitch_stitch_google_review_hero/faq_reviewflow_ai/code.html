How does the AI sentiment analysis work?

expand_more
Our proprietary AI engine uses advanced natural language processing (NLP) to read and interpret the emotional tone behind every review. It automatically categorizes feedback into positive, neutral, or negative sentiments, allowing you to quickly identify trends and areas requiring immediate attention without manual triage.

Is it safe to connect my Google Business Profile?

expand_more
Absolutely. We use Google's official API with OAuth 2.0 authentication. We never store your Google password, and we only request the specific permissions needed to read and reply to reviews on your behalf. You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

Can I customize the automated AI responses?

expand_more
Yes, you have full control. You can set strict guidelines, define your brand's voice (e.g., professional, casual, empathetic), and even require manual approval for responses generated for 1-star or 2-star reviews before they are published to Google.

What happens if I exceed my monthly review limit?

expand_more
If you approach your limit, we'll notify you in advance. If you exceed it, new reviews will still be collected and stored, but AI auto-responses and deep sentiment analysis will be paused until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade your plan. We never block access to your raw data.
